# Curriculum Map

This project follows a fundamentals-first physics path inspired by Susan
Rigetti's self-study structure: learn the math and equations, solve problems,
then build simulations that make the equations visible.

## Current Track: Mechanics

Implemented:

- Projectile motion
- Gravity
- Quadratic drag
- Explicit Euler
- Semi-implicit Euler
- Energy diagnostics
- Simple pendulum
- Small-angle pendulum approximation

Next:

- RK4 integrator
- Analytic projectile comparison
- Regression tests
- Spring and mass experiments
- Orbit lab

## Later Tracks

- Waves
- Fields and electromagnetism
- Thermal and statistical mechanics
- Quantum toy models
